Conclusion Of Industry 4.0

  1. 1. Industry 4.0 has proved to be both the curse and the boon.It has flourished several jobs and also threatened many.

  3. 3. Industry 4.0 is definitely a revolutionary approach to manufacturing techniques. The concept will push global manufacturers to a new level of optimization and productivity. Not only that, but customers will also enjoy a new level of personally customized products that may have never been available before. As mentioned above, the economic rewards are immense.
  4. 4. However, there are still many challenges that need to be tackled systematically to ensure a smooth transition. This needs to be the focus of large corporations and governments alike. Pushing research and experimentation in such fields are essential.
  5. 5. There is no better time than now to start integrating smart manufacturing into your business production chain.
  6. 6. While speculations regarding privacy, security, and employment need more study, the overall picture is promising. Such approach to manufacturing industries is truly revolutionary.
